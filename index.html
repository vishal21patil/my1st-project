Raigad fort

Raigad is a hill fort situated about 25 km from Mahad in the Raigad district and stands 2,851 feet above the sea level.


Pratapgad fort

Pratapgad Fort is a mountain fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fort is at a distance of 24 km from the hill station of Mahabaleshwar.


Lohagad fort

Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj captured it in 1648 AD, but he was forced to surrender it to the Mughals in 1665 AD by the Treaty of Purandar.


Janjira fort

Janjira is a colloquial and a more localized form of the Arabic word Jazeera that means island.


Shivneri fort

Folks travelling by public transport will need to walk for about 2 kms from Junnar to get to the base fo the fort.
